Fishing trips in France

Thanks to our close cooperation with unique natural hunting resorts and reserves in the suburbs of Paris and other suburbs in France, enjoy wild hunting trips with us for birds, deer, rabbits and all kinds of prey, whether with family and friends or alone in complete harmony with the distinctive nature

Whether you are a beginner or a professional fisherman, we will provide you with a program lined up according to your desires and according to your tastes

Thanks to the leadership of our partners in the field of hunting trips and their accurate knowledge of the best hunting reserves, you can choose and guarantee the hunting of any type of game available in France with pleasure and safety

The trip program provides you with hotel reservation and transportation by private car from the hotel to the hunting reserve, meal. In addition, the owner of the reserve can prepare and clean the game

Our trips are also suitable for non-fishermen by providing an exploration, cultural or sports program with a personal escort. Thanks to the specialization of our partners, we can provide many options to meet all requests
