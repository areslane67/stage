reception and transportation services VIP

Thanks to our close cooperation and constant interaction with service companies at airports, we offer passengers from Paris Charles de Gaulle CDG, Paris-Orly and other airports on request, VIP reception services from arrival and exit from the plane door and passing through the fast lane, until the ride in the limousine to the hotel or Residence address and vice versa

We provide you with transportation services with the latest and most luxurious types of limousines of all sizes upon request.

Access Services

The concierge welcomes you from the exit gate or on the bridge of the plane with a sign with your name and is responsible for all stages of arrival

  • Carrying cabin baggage
  • Facilitate passage for passport control
  • Baggage retrieval and carry-on
  • Accompany you up to your own transport vehicle

go services

Reception upon arrival at the airport by the concierge and taking care of all stages of going

  • carry luggage
  • Tax Refund Assistance
  • Registration help
  • Facilitate passage to all checkpoints
  • Escort to the door of the plane
